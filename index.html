Who I Am

I am a “guardian of data” with a passion for cloud computing along with the flow and security of data. While completing my MIS degree, I discovered a love for SQL with its logic, data structures, and data flow diagrams as I coded new databases and developed complex queries. I studied cloud computing and how it solves many problems that small businesses face with managing their data safely and securely. But, how can I help others to find business solutions through cloud computing while enjoying my passion for building projects? As an ambitious problem solver, I pursued this question to discover another passion for coding and learning new program languages.
